# Insurance Portfolio Analysis (PySpark + Databricks)

## Project Overview
This project simulates a large-scale insurance portfolio and analyzes claim patterns, portfolio risk, and profitability using **PySpark in Databricks**.

A synthetic dataset of **1,000,000 insurance policies** was generated to model real-world insurance operations and evaluate claim behavior, premium revenue, and loss ratios.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- PySpark
- Databricks
- Python
- Spark SQL

---

## Project Workflow
1. Generated synthetic insurance policy data.
2. Simulated claims for 2025 and 2026 based on business rules.
3. Performed analytical queries to evaluate claim costs and portfolio profitability.
4. Calculated key insurance metrics such as loss ratio and future claim liability.

---

## Key Analysis Performed
- Total premium collected in 2024
- Monthly claim cost analysis for 2025 and 2026
- Claim cost to premium ratio by policy tenure
- Claim ratio by policy purchase month
- Estimation of future claim liability
- Premium earned vs expected premium

---

## Key Insights
- Premium revenue remains stable due to evenly distributed policy sales.
- Claim activity begins mainly in 2025 when policies become active.
- Shorter tenure policies tend to be more profitable due to lower risk exposure.
- An increase in claim frequency can significantly affect portfolio profitability.
